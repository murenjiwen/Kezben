This is the NTU-Microsoft-Kinect-HandGesture dataset, which contains the color image and the corresponding depth map obtained from the Kinect sensor.

It contains 10 gestures taken from 10 subjects, and for each gesture, there are 10 variations. Thus, there are 10*10*10 = 1000 cases.

Please refer to the paper below:
"Robust Hand Gesture Recognition based on Finger-Earth Mover’s Distance with a Commodity Depth Camera", Proc. of ACM Multimedia 2011, Zhou Ren, Junsong Yuan, and Zhengyou Zhang.

"Robust Hand Gesture Recognition with Kinect Sensor", Proc. of ACM Multimedia 2011, (Demo paper) Zhou Ren, Jingjing Meng, Junsong Yuan, and Zhengyou Zhang.
